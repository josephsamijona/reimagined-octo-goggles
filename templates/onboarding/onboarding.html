How Assignments Work

  • You receive an assignment notification via email
  • Accept or decline directly from the email link
  • Confirmed assignments appear on your dashboard calendar
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to on-site assignments
  • Mark assignments as complete when finished
📊

Your Dashboard

Once onboarded, you'll have access to your personal dashboard where you can:

  • View upcoming assignments and your schedule
  • Track your earnings
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Access signed contracts and documents
💵

Getting Paid

  • Payments are processed via ACH direct deposit
  • Track all your earnings from the dashboard
  • Year-end tax documents (1099) provided automatically

You'll set up your bank details during this onboarding process.
